About the Portal

A clean platform for meaningful admission insights

This portal is designed to analyze JoSAA seat allotment statistics for IITs from 2016 to 2022. It transforms raw admission data into a structured and visually engaging experience.

The goal is to help users understand branch popularity, institute-wise trends, seat movement, competitiveness, diversity of offerings, and changing admission patterns over time through charts, tables, and interactive comparisons.
